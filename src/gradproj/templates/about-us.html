What is ABET?

nonprofit, ISO 9001 certified organization that accredits college and university programs in applied and natural science, computing, engineering and engineering technology.




What are the learning outcomes of Computer Science according to ABET?

SAnalyze a complex computing problem, design, implement, and evaluate a computing program, communicate effectively in a variety of professional contexts, recognize professional responsibilities and make informed judgments in computing practice and function effectively as a member or leader



How Study Source works?

Study Source was developed over machine learning techniques for recommending the courses to be taken in the curriculum based on a questionnaire filled by a student, in addition to helpful resources and links that cover the material of courses. The questionnaire was built according to ABET learning outcomes related to Computer Science.



What is the curriculum tree shown by Study Source?

Study Source demonstrates a tree that represents the different courses in the curriculum of Computer Science in a hierarchical format according to the years in which these courses can be taken. Study Source ranks these courses from "Strongly recommended" to "Weakly recommended". The color of each clickable course in the tree shows how it has been ranked.
